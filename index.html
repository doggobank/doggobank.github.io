Zero application cost

Frequently Asked



What is this?

This is a Decentralised Bank designed to Bank all Projects on the BlockChain, from Fair Launch Initial Liquidity to loans for already established projects and individual investors who aim to keep their money at work.

Inspiration?

The idea was born in October 2022 during the soccer betting hype, upon trying to fair launch a project. Only to realize the ETH in hand needed to be ampler to attract investors, compared to rival projects. That initial launch was canceled, and the idea for Doge Bank was born. Fair launches are here to stay, and Doge Bank introduces an alt approach, besides private sales, that attracts investors and guarantee repayment without giving them tokens in exchange.

Who can get funded?

Any new project that's launching can get funded.

Whats the procedure?

A project must use our solidity functions/interface in their Smart Contract. A guide will be made soon for developers. Once implemented correctly, you can apply on our Smart Contract. You pay nothing to get funded.

How the Bank funds these projects?

The Bank owns a Fixed Term Deposits Vault for ETH; from this, we move the capital to the Meme Bank Smart Contract to fund projects and charge a fixed interest to all projects funded. From the profits after repayment, we sent dividends back to the Vault for depositors to withdraw as passive income.

How Individual Wallets funds these projects?

Like on any launchpad, wallets can discover projects looking for Initial Liquidity or Loans. They wait for projects to be vetted by the Bank and approved; crowdfunding can begin once approved.

How do Investors/Wallets get repaid

Each project that applies for funding has to implement our in-house designed function, which takes from tax revenue directly before sending any money to any other wallets like marketing, auto liquidity adding, business development, reflections, etc. Repayments to our investors come first. With a 20% interest. The higher the volume, the quicker the investors get their capital back.

What guarantee do investors have for repayment?

In addition to implementing our in-house repayment function, we expect to see the smart contract of those projects implements our "receiveFunding" function that uses Uniswaps addLiquidity function with our LP Vault smart contract address as the recipient of the LP tokens directly from Uniswap as collateral.

Are there costs for applying projects?

Expect a cost of Audit if your smart contract is extended and carries complex other functions that could: mint, stop trading, stop taxes, stop repayments. Only upon going through our Audit partners and passing the Audit will the project get approved for funding.

How does a project get a loan?

Established projects with good liquidity are the only ones that qualify for a loan. The vetting process is lengthier than for initial liquidity funding. We expect the applicant to first deposit their project's LP tokens in our LP Vault and proceed to pass the matching address to our "requestFunding" function during application.

Can individuals get loans?

Only projects can get loans. We only recognize LP tokens as sufficient collateral, so only projects implementing taxed transactions in their Smart Contract. They may face cashflow shortages in their bid to expand the project's utility, which they know will bring more investors and, in turn, more volume; that is our target market.

What happens when repayments fail?

We hold the debtor's LP tokens in our LP Vault as collateral. Upon failure to repay, we pull the liquidity of that project to retrieve the capital contributed by investors. We then deposit it into the repayments pool for that project so investors can withdraw their money back.

Should we all deposit to our Fixed Term Deposits Vault?

You can keep your funds in your wallets and contribute in a funding round directly from your wallet on a project of your choosing. The Vault is meant for investors who don't have the time needed to chase Fair Launches or Loan requests.

How does this Bank protocol work?

Our protocol is made up of 4 Smart Contracts all specifically designed to communicate with each other only. High security measures and standards are implemented across the protocol to ensure that customer funds are safe, whether they are in the Fixed Term Deposits Vault, or you are connecting your wallet to the Bank funding smart contract to participate in a raise.

Future plans?

The payments platform is also in the cards based on NFTs linked to the Bank Fixed Term Deposits Vault.
