Fund Fair Launches
We crowd fund Fair Launchinitial liquidity, funders get repaid directly from the funded project's taxes. I.e Contribute $100, receive $120 in a few hours from trading start.

Secure Interface
We want to provide a safe enviroment for our investors to fund projects in. As such: Projects that are funded by our contract have to implement our interface correctly to guarantee repayment.

Zero application cost
Projects that apply for fair launch funding or crypto loans pay zero cost. They only have to repay with a fixed interest.

Smart Funding with collateral.
When a project's Initial Liquidity is funded through our contract, the LP tokens minted go directly to our LP Vault. We recognise LP tokens as collateral.
Collaterised Crypto Loans
Loan your idle money to projects that need operational cash, collaterised by LP tokens. If a project implements taxed transactions then it qualifies for a loan if their volume is deemed sufficient to generate repayment money.

Deposit money into our fixed term vault
Projects or individuals can deposit money into our vault. The bank like any other, then uses this as capital to finance fair launches or issue collaterised loans, on behalf of depositors.

LP Tokens custodial services
We offer LP custodial services. During which you are also eligible to borrow money from the Bank using the same tokens in our custody.

Grand scheme
Hundreds of fair launches happen each day, with almost all of them implementing taxed transactions. They can generate revenue. Our platform aims to finance them whilst allowing our contributors to also make money quickly with each launch or loan..

Frequently Asked



What is this?

This is a Decentralised Bank designed to bank all Projects on the BlockChain. From Fair Launch Initial Liquidity to loans for already established projects, and individual investors who aim to keep their money at work.

Inspiration?

The idea was born in October 2022 during the soccer betting hype, upon trying to fair launch a project. Only to realise the ETH in hand wasnt ample enough to attract investors, compared to rival projects. That launch was cancelled and the idea for Doge Bank was born. Fair Launches are here to stay, and Doge Bank introduces an alt approach, besides private sales, that attracts investors and guarantee repayment without giving them tokens in exchange?

Who can get funded?

Any new project thats launching can get funded.

Whats the procedure?

A project simply needs to make use of our solidity functions/interface in their Smart Contract. A guide will be made soon for developers. Once implemented correctly you can go ahead and apply on our Smart Contract. You pay nothing to get funded.

How the Bank funds these projects?

The Bank owns a Fixed Term Deposits Vault for ETH, from this we move capital to the Meme Bank Smart Contract to fund projects, and charge a fixed interest to all projects funded. From the profits after repayment, we sent dividents back to the Vault for depositors to withdraw as passive income.

How Individual Wallets funds these projects?

Like on any launchpad, wallets can come discover projects looking for Initial Liquidity or Loans. They simply wait for projects to be vetted by the Bank and be approved, once approved the crowd funding can begin.

How do Investors/Wallets get repaid

Each project that applies for funding has to implement our in-house designed function, that takes from tax revenue directly before sending any money to any other wallets like marketing, auto liquidity adding, business development, reflections etc. Repayments to our investors come first. With a 20% interest. The higher the volume the quicker the investors get their capital back.

What guarantee do investors have for repayment?

In addition to implementing our in-house repayment function, we expect to see the smart contract of those projects implement our "receiveFunding" function that uses Uniswaps addLiquidity function with our LP Vault smart contract address as the recipient of the LP tokens directly from Uniswap as collateral.

Are there costs for applying projects?

Expect a cost of Audit if your smart contract is long and carry complex other functions that could possibly: mint, stop trading, stop taxes, stop repayments. Only upon going through our Audit partners and passing the audit, will the project get approved for funding.

How does a project get a loan?

Already established projects with good liquidity are the only ones that qualify for a loan. The vetting process is lengthier than for initial liquidity funding. We expect the applicant to first deposit their project's LP tokens in our LP Vault and proceed to pass the matching address to our "requestFunding" function during application.

Can individuals get loans?

Only projects can get loans. We only recognise LP tokens as sufficient collateral. Projects that implement taxed transactions in their Smart Contract. Its possible they may face cashflow shortages in their bid to expand the projects utility, a utility they know will bring more investors and in turn more volume, that is our target market.

What happens when repayments fail?

We hold the debtors LP tokens in our LP Vault as collateral. Upon failure to repay, we simply pull the liquidity of that project in order to retrive the capital contributed by investors. We then go on to deposit it into the repayments pool for that project so investors can withdraw their capital back.

Should we all deposit to our Fixed Term Deposits Vault?

You can keep your funds in your wallets, and contribute in a funding round directly from your wallet on a project of your choosing. The vault is meant for investors who do not have time to chase all Fair Launches or Loan requests.

How does this Bank protocol work?

Our protocol is made up of 4 Smart Contracts all specifically designed to communicate with each other only. High security measures and standards are implemented across the protocol to ensure that customer funds are safe, whether they are in the Fixed Term Deposits Vault, or you are connecting your wallet to the Bank funding smart contract to participate in a raise.

Future plans?

Payments platform is also on the cards based on NFTs linked to the Bank Fixed Term Deposits Vault.
