What is Doge Bank?
  • Doge Bank was forged in the popular meme fire of fair launches.
  • We saw a gap in the market where liquidity is in short supply yet in so much demand.
  • New projects facing liquidity issues die off, those that auto add LP have a chance.
  • Private sales have their own downside, as presalers dump or project owners rug.
  • Liquidity miners who normally feared impermanant loss now fear losing it all.
  • The solution was to create Doge Bank, a decentralised platform that Bankrolls all projects in need of Initial Liquidity or loans.
  • We aim to achieve risk free liquidity mining, where investors crowd fund the initial liquidity of a project for a fixed return, instead of doing presales for project tokens in return. Presales are dead.
  • In addition, we recognise LP tokens as sound collateral, thus opening the door to P2P collaterised loans. Our collateral vault accepts: LP tokens, Stable Coins, NFTs.
  • We rely on transaction taxes for repayment directly to our investors, with interest per dollar capital, per project financed.
  • LP tokens for any financed project go directly to our vault (f### around on repayments and find out).

    • We are in the money business: We fund fair launches for interest. We issue loans for interest. We offer LP custodial services. We also accept deposits for fixed interest. We aim to create a risk free investment engine for our investors. Fair launches and memes are not going away anytime soon, neither are the liquidity issues in the market. The goal is to support projects whilst also profiting, all parties benefit in the end.
    Our Banking Protocol relies on 4 smart contracts.
    Beta testing will be available soon on Goerli testnet, after our token launch.


    Our Protocol

    In no particular order, current features available:
  • Wallet issued loans.
  • Bank issued loans.
  • Wallet funded Initial Liquidity direct to the LP of the fair launch.
  • Bank funded Initial Liquidity, also direct to LP.
  • LP tokens vault, which acts as our collateral vault.
  • Fixed term deposits cash vault.
  • LP custodial services vault.
  • Investors just like on launchpads, come to crowd fund a project launch for a fixed return.
  • The Bank can also fund projects on behalf of depositors and sends dividents back to the cash vault.
  • The cash vault is open only on reconciliation days, during other days: only the current depositors will benefit from dividents.
  • Bank can move funding between cash vault and Bank funding contract.
  • The bank can accept capital into its current account on the Doge Bank smart contract.
  • A capital repayment function repays capital to the cash vault, dividents send separately.
  • Fair Launch Funding function to point to the Smart Contract and LP of the requesting project.
  • Fair Launch Funding function to issue loan to the receiving address of the requesting project.
  • Only requesting project owner can apply for fair launch funding or collateralized project loans.
  • A project can only receive initial liquidity funding once, stored in mapping by project address.
  • Projects can apply repeatedly if denied the first time, there is no limit.
  • Loan applications happen continously and on condition to the applicants previous loans.
  • All repayments are done with fixed interest directly to the funders: bank or individual investors.
  • All applications should meet criteria: correct project details, correct addresses provided (incl router and factory), correct implementation of our interface/functions in applicants smart contract, a mint function only in the receive funding function of the applicantS SC.
  • Projects choose when applying to be funded or loaned by: Bank or Crowd funding (wallets).
  • All applications are vetted and then stamped an approval or denial. Re-applications allowed.
  • The requester must implement our interface/functions in their smart contract correctly in order to receive initial liquidity.
  • We do not fund directly to the applicants Liquidity Pool based on just Factory address and Project address.
  • All stamped requests/applications are automatically open for funding after.
  • LP tokens collected as collateral during fair launch funding have to be manually mapped by admins or project owner: using returned address from receiving SC interface implementation.
  • Project settlement function: admin after removing liquidity from defaulted projects LP can repay proceeds direct to Project struct.
  • Each investor wallet can track the projects they have funded or loaned.
  • An array of all funded projects ever is kept: for both bank and individuals collective.
  • A current account is kept by the Bank, funded directly by vault or repayments.
  • LP tokens vault has capability for custodial services outside of loans and funding fair launches.
  • Token holders of the Banks tokens earn reflections, including from dividents from the Banks current account.
  • These reflections can be reassigned by each investor to a beneficiary wallet of choice: i.e. add siblings wallet, charity wallet
  • P2P funders or wallets can withdraw their capital and interest manually, its kept on our contract under the funded project's address.
  • Funded projects can have limited or unlimited cap on repayment amount.
  • Projects that borrow larger amounts will have unlimited or longer caps: i.e. they can pay initial capital back then continued taxes for agreed days.
  • This is still testnet, more functionality can be added, and some functions adjusted after or during Beta testing.
